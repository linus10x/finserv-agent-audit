## Diagnostic — standard pricing

The Diagnostic engagement is a 4-week scoped review of the buyer's autonomous-agent governance posture against the Autonomy Ladder™ framework. Deliverables:

- A control-matrix walkthrough of the buyer's current state against the framework's six core patterns + six FSI-specific governance gates (or, for CRE buyers, the cre-agent-audit pattern set)
- A gap report identifying the top three governance gaps in the buyer's current posture, with documented evidence and proposed remediation paths
- A one-page executive summary suitable for the buyer's audit committee, internal-audit team, or chief risk officer
- A one-hour debrief call with the buyer's executive sponsor

**Standard price: $5,000 USD per engagement.** Fixed-fee, payable on engagement-letter execution. Engagement window: 4 weeks from kickoff to debrief. Re-engagement (a follow-on Diagnostic on a different scope) priced at standard $5,000.

## Cohort-Zero pilot — first 5 logos

**Pilot price: $1,000 USD per engagement for the first 5 logos.**

The Cohort-Zero pilot is a recruiting tool to seed the framework's case-study and reference base. Five (5) Diagnostic engagements at the pilot rate, with case-study rights, in exchange for the pilot price.

### Eligibility

The pilot is open to executives in the following roles at the following organization types:

- **Private Capital:** Operating partners or executives-in-residence at private-equity firms with ≥ $1B AUM and active portfolio-company AI initiatives; chief investment officers or chief operating officers at family offices with ≥ $250M AUM; chief technology officers, chief information officers, or chief risk officers at wealth-management platforms with ≥ $5B AUC; buy-side AI program leads at hedge funds, asset managers, or insurance companies' investment arms
- **Commercial Real Estate (sibling `cre-agent-audit` framework):** chief data officers, chief technology officers, chief risk officers, or general counsel at multifamily / single-family-rental / commercial-real-estate operators with active AI initiatives in tenant screening, rent pricing, or property management
- **Other regulated AI surfaces:** chief AI officers, chief compliance officers, or general counsel at US-regulated firms (banks, broker-dealers, insurance carriers, registered investment advisers, fintech under federal or state supervision) with active autonomous-agent initiatives

Self-funded individuals, vendors building competing products, and academic researchers are not eligible for the Cohort-Zero pilot; the framework's open-source license already covers their use cases at no cost.

### Case-study rights

In exchange for the pilot price, the engaging organization grants the framework the following rights:

- **Anonymized case-study publication.** A 2-3 page anonymized case study describing the engagement scope, the top three governance gaps surfaced, the proposed remediation paths, and the organization's high-level response (without naming the organization). The case study is reviewed for sign-off by the organization's general counsel before publication. The GC sign-off level is the ceiling on what is published; the framework does not publish anything the GC does not approve.
- **One-paragraph testimonial.** A one-paragraph testimonial attributed to a named individual (CTO / CRO / GC / CISO / Chief AI Officer / Chief Data Officer of the engaging organization), subject to the individual's and the GC's sign-off. The testimonial may be attributed by name and title, or attributed anonymously by role only ("CRO at a top-10 US wealth-management platform"), at the engaging organization's election.
- **Reference call (optional).** The engaging organization may agree, at its sole election, to take 1-2 reference calls per quarter from prospective Diagnostic clients evaluating the framework. The reference-call commitment is not a condition of the pilot price; it is offered as a separately-elected option.

### Anti-dilution

- **Hard cap of 5 logos.** Logo 6 onward pays standard $5,000.
- **First-come, first-served.** Pilot slot is reserved on engagement-letter execution, not on initial inquiry.
- **Pilot offer expires when full.** When all 5 slots are reserved, the pilot offer is taken down from the autonomy-ladder.io/services page within 5 business days. Inquiries received after the offer expires are routed to the standard Diagnostic price.
- **One pilot slot per logo.** A single organization (or group of organizations under common control) is eligible for one pilot slot, not multiple.
- **No price-match guarantee.** Once an engagement closes at pilot price, the engaging organization has no claim to price-match on future engagements; subsequent engagements with the same organization price at standard $5,000 Diagnostic / $40,000 Audit / $15,000 per quarter Retainer.

### Pilot-slot counter

The autonomy-ladder.io/services page displays a public counter: **"Pilot logos remaining: N of 5"**, updated within 5 business days of each new engagement-letter execution.

## Path beyond Diagnostic

Pilot and standard Diagnostic engagements that produce a buyer-side decision to proceed flow to the next two tiers on the standard advisory ladder:

| Tier | Scope | Standard price |
|---|---|---|
| Audit | 8-12 week scoped engagement: comprehensive control-matrix design, evidence collection support, regulator-facing artifact preparation, executive-committee briefings | $40,000 USD per engagement, fixed fee |
| Retainer | Ongoing advisory: 4 hours per month of senior advisory time, plus emergency-line access for regulator inquiries, plus quarterly executive briefings | $15,000 USD per quarter, billed quarterly |

The Audit and Retainer tiers are NOT subject to the Cohort-Zero pricing. The pilot price applies only to the entry-tier Diagnostic.

## Frequently asked questions (publish on autonomy-ladder.io/services)

**Q: Why $1K for a $5K engagement?**
A: The Cohort-Zero pilot is a recruiting tool. Case studies and reference calls from named clients are the framework's most valuable marketing artifact, and the $4K price differential is paid in exchange for the case-study and testimonial rights described above. The pilot expires at 5 logos; subsequent engagements price at standard $5K.

**Q: What if I'm eligible but the pilot is full?**
A: Standard Diagnostic price applies. The framework's open-source license also remains freely available for self-directed adoption with no engagement at all.

**Q: Can I get the pilot price for Audit or Retainer?**
A: No. The Cohort-Zero pilot price applies only to the entry-tier Diagnostic. Audit ($40K) and Retainer ($15K/q) price at standard rates.

**Q: Can I negotiate the case-study and testimonial rights?**
A: Yes. The case-study rights are at GC sign-off level by default; if the engaging organization has a more conservative public-disclosure posture, the rights can be negotiated to a higher anonymization level. The testimonial is opt-in by name or by role at the engaging organization's election. Reference-call commitment is opt-in at the engaging organization's election. The pilot price is contingent on at minimum the anonymized case-study right.

**Q: Who delivers the engagement?**
A: Kunjar Bhaduri, the author of the framework. Engagements are delivered personally; sub-contracting to third parties is not used.

**Q: How do I apply for a Cohort-Zero pilot slot?**
A: Contact the author at kunjarbhaduri@gmail.com with subject prefix **`[COHORT ZERO]`**. Include the engaging organization, the engagement scope, and the executive sponsor. Author confirms receipt within 2 business days and proposes engagement-letter terms within 5 business days.
